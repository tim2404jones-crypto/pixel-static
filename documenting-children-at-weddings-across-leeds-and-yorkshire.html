Documenting Children 'Stealing the Show' at Weddings by Tim Christian Jones

From cheeky little pageboys to shy flower girls twirling at the edge of the room, these moments appear when kids feel welcome and relaxed. I like to blend in gently so they forget the camera is even there — and that’s when the real magic happens.

It’s never just about photographing children being cute; it’s about capturing the spirit of your day through everyone who matters — big and small.

Here’s a gallery filled with joy, mischief and those unscripted memories you’ll treasure for years to come. If you'd like moments like these to link your Leeds wedding story—I am Tim Christian Jones—a Passionate Leeds wedding photographer

Couples love the way I am able to capture the little ones whom often 'Steal the Show' at weddings. Because my wedding photography is unedited, and pure in this era of needless editing, I believe in the timeless nature of real photos. I am also happy to travel wherever asked. Likewise, I love what I do, and hope you see this in my extensive and representative Portfolio.
