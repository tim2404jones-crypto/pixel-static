Feydrah & Dean’s Summer Wedding Chevin Country Park Hotel, Otley

Feydrah & Dean’s summer wedding at Chevin Country Park Hotel & Spa unfolded in the heart of the Chevin hills, surrounded by woodland, water, and the calm of late August light.

Chevin Country Park Hotel & Spa has a quiet, distinctive character — set among woodland and water above Otley, where the landscape feels open, calm, and naturally connected to the flow of a wedding day. It’s a place I love photographing because the surroundings gently shape the atmosphere without ever overwhelming it.

My approach here is the same as across all my weddings — stay unobtrusive, stay present, and document the day as it unfolds. Real moments, honest emotion, and a story that feels true to how it was experienced.
