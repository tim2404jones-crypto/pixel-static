An Intimate Blue-Sky Wedding in Leeds! Sunflowers, Cloudless Sky, and Emotive Family Moments

Leeds Civic Hall Wedding Photography — Gemma & Danny

Gemma and Danny planned an intimate DIY wedding, greeted by clear blue skies and warm sunshine. Sunflowers in hand, pale blue suits, and light that felt quietly made for them.

Their day began at Leeds Civic Hall, where a relaxed, happy ceremony held just the right touch of emotion.

Wedding Photography at Leeds Civic Hall

After the ceremony and a few photographs, guests made their way to Lost & Found at the Leeds Club. I stayed back with Gemma and Danny for a few relaxed, candid portraits on Millennium Square, bathed in perfect sunshine.

We paused briefly for a drink before heading over, catching quiet moments as they walked along Albion Place and arrived at the Leeds Club.

There was time for photos, easy conversation, and an intimate wedding breakfast upstairs in the Boardroom.

A few days later, I joined them again at Lost & Found for their evening reception. More friends and family gathered, the room filled with relaxed conversation, followed by speeches and their first dance — all unfolding naturally, captured in the available light.

Tim did our wedding photos and we couldn't be happier with the results. Quite easily the best photos we have ever seen and sent to us the next day! We can't believe there is no editing and they are simply superb photography skills. Tim really has a talent of knowing how to capture the moment giving a natural feel to the photos with no awkward poses , just smiles and joy captured all round . Highly recommend Tim for any photography , he was a pleasure to have with us on the day and has given us such special photos to remember our day by.

Gemma & Danny
