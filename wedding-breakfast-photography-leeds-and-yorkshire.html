The Wedding Breakfast — Documentary Style

The wedding breakfast is where everything softens a little. People relax, stories get told, glasses are raised, and the room fills with laughter.

I’m not looking to stage anything or turn it into something it’s not. I just quietly document what’s already there — the real moments, the atmosphere, the feeling of it all.

Three wedding breakfast stories. 150+ photographs. Scroll at your own pace, or click an image and let it play.

If this feels like your kind of thing, you can see more of my work or get in touch.

Lowri & Tim’s wedding breakfast was held in a quiet memorial hall, filled with soft, natural light. Nothing over the top, just a calm space where everything could unfold naturally.

There were gentle conversations, bursts of laughter, and that lovely in-between feeling as the day settled. The kind of atmosphere where moments don’t need directing — they just happen.
