Chevin Country Park Hotel Wedding — Kyra & Matt’s August Celebration

  • By Tim Christian Jones
  • 25 May 2025

Kyra and Matt’s wedding day unfolded across two beautiful Yorkshire settings, filled with sunshine, laughter, and genuine emotion.

Their ceremony took place at St Oswald’s Church in Guiseley before everyone travelled on to Chevin Country Park Hotel, tucked among the woodland just outside Otley. The shift in pace felt natural — from quiet, heartfelt moments in the church to a relaxed, joyful celebration in the Chevin hills.

This late August wedding carried an easy elegance throughout, shaped by light, movement, and the people at its heart — exactly the kind of day that suits a calm, documentary approach.

Morning Preparations & Arrivals

I arrived just in time to catch the final moments of preparation at Chevin Country Park Hotel. The bridesmaids wore soft pink dresses, framed by the shaded lodge and the quiet woodland beyond. Kyra and her father shared a calm, heartfelt smile beside the white Rolls Royce, parked in dappled August light outside the lodge.

Later, at St Oswald’s Church in Guiseley, the stone walls glowed warmly as guests began to arrive. Matt and his groomsmen gathered at the entrance, laughing together in the sunshine, while the bridesmaids followed soon after — relaxed, joyful, and ready for what was to come.

Inside, gentle light filtered across the pews. Family and friends settled into a quiet stillness, sharing soft conversation and smiles before the ceremony began.

Ceremony at St Oswald’s Church, Guiseley

Kyra arrived with her father and bridesmaids, all beaming in the late summer light. The white Rolls Royce and the stone façade of St Oswald’s made a beautiful backdrop as they stepped forward together.

As the ceremony began, the atmosphere softened. A proud smile from Matt, a quiet exchange of glances, and the easy rhythm of vows unfolding naturally. Light streamed through the stained glass as rings were exchanged and family reactions flickered gently across the pews.

After a joyful hymn, the couple stepped back out into the sunshine, sharing a kiss on the church steps before greeting friends and family with warmth and ease.

Confetti, Family & Celebration Outside the Church

The churchyard became a place of laughter and movement. Confetti filled the air as Kyra and Matt walked the path together, followed closely by their two dogs — instantly stealing hearts.

We gathered everyone beneath the trees for a full group photograph, then took relaxed family portraits in the shade. A bouquet toss brought another burst of energy, with Kyra sending hers skyward and Matt raising laughs by tossing a smaller one to the gents, who scattered in mock panic.

Nothing felt rushed. Everything unfolded as it should.

Quiet Portraits Between Church and Reception

Before travelling on, Kyra and Matt stayed behind for a few quiet portraits in the church grounds. These moments were completely unposed — just the two of them pausing, breathing, and letting the day catch up.

They were gentle, honest frames. The kind that don’t announce themselves, but mean more with time.

Arrival, Woodland Portraits & Group Moments at Chevin Country Park Hotel

When everyone arrived at Chevin Country Park Hotel, the pace stayed relaxed. Before heading inside, we took a gentle walk through the woodland — soft light, still trees, and space to settle into the next part of the day. No direction, no staging, just movement and quiet connection.

Nearby, guests gathered naturally for a full group photograph, waving and smiling as they came together. One of my favourite moments followed soon after — friends leaning over the balcony, waving down below, their reflections mirrored perfectly in the lake. A fleeting, joyful scene that captured the atmosphere beautifully.

Wedding Breakfast & Speeches

As the wedding breakfast began, the room filled with easy conversation and warmth. Glasses clinked, food was shared, and the sound level rose gently as everyone settled in.

The speeches brought laughter and quieter reactions in equal measure — smiles exchanged across tables, heads bowed in moments of reflection, and the kind of honest responses that unfold best when nothing feels staged. Everything moved at its own pace, exactly as the day had from the start.

Evening Celebrations at Chevin Country Park Hotel & Spa

As evening settled in, guests drifted back outside into the last of the light. The cake was cut with laughter and easy smiles, followed by the first dance — unforced, unpolished, and full of warmth.

The room softened as people gathered closer, the energy lifting gently as the night began to take shape. Nothing felt staged or rushed — just a natural ending to a day that had unfolded at its own pace.

Closing Thoughts

Kyra and Matt’s wedding unfolded with an easy rhythm — from the quiet intimacy of St Oswald’s Church to the woodland calm of Chevin Country Park Hotel. Light, movement, and genuine connection shaped the day, allowing moments to surface naturally without interruption.

This wedding sits among a beautiful collection of stories photographed around the Chevin — from woodland settings to lakeside celebrations — gathered together in my Chevin weddings archive.
