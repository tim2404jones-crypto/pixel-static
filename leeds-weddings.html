A Collection of Leeds Weddings

The very first wedding I photographed, back in 2004, was here in Leeds — a local church ceremony followed by a small marquee reception in the couple’s garden. Years later, I still find myself documenting weddings across the city in completely different ways. One recent celebration at Leeds Civic Hall unfolded beneath the bluest skies I’ve ever photographed, perfectly matching the couple’s blue suit theme and bright sunflower bouquet details.

A growing collection of real Leeds weddings, photographed across the city in a natural documentary style.

As a Leeds wedding photographer , I’ve always felt a real responsibility to document each wedding as a true sequence of memories — real moments, atmosphere, and emotion preserved in a way that lets couples relive the day for years to come.

Musings of a Leeds Wedding Photographer

One Leeds wedding I still remember vividly was beautifully simple — a ceremony at Leeds Town Hall, followed by a meal at Little Tokyo just a short walk away. I always love it when couples bring their own personality into the day rather than following expectations. They had shared meals there together before and wanted that part of the city, and that memory, woven naturally into their wedding story.

I was even invited to sit and eat with them for a while. I still remember the desserts arriving in decorative bowls sculpted from ice, with mint leaves frozen inside. Small details like that tend to stay with me.

Photographing weddings in Leeds has never really felt like work to me. I feel genuinely grateful to document so many different celebrations across Leeds and nearby areas, each with its own atmosphere, people, and story. If you connect with the way I see weddings and moments naturally unfolding, I’d love to photograph your Leeds wedding in a way that feels completely personal to you.

One of the things I love most about weddings in Leeds is the variety the city offers — grand civic spaces, modern light-filled venues, hidden gardens, and countryside views just outside the centre. Every wedding feels visually different, yet always rooted in the same warmth.

This collection continues to grow with more real stories, moments, and memories photographed across Leeds and nearby areas.
