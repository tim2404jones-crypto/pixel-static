Wedding Photography at Chilli Barn, Otley — Helen & Adam’s Summer Festivities

  • By Tim Christian Jones
  • 09 August 2025

Helen & Adam’s Chilli Barn, Otley Wedding

Helen & Adam’s wedding at Chilli Barn, tucked away beside Chevin Forest Park near Otley, surrounded by open fields and the edge of Chevin Forest, it was a day bursting with colour, laughter, and love. As a Leeds wedding photographer, I loved capturing their relaxed, joyful moments at this laid-back Yorkshire barn venue.

If you like natural, unposed wedding photography around Leeds and West Yorkshire, this gives a feel for how I document a day — quietly, honestly, and as it unfolds.

Scroll on for a glimpse into the beautiful, natural moments from their day.

The Wedding Ceremony at The Chilli Barn, Otley — Captured by a Leeds Wedding Photographer

Surrounded by open countryside near Otley, with Chevin Forest rising nearby, Helen and Adam’s ceremony at The Chilli Barn felt relaxed, warm, and full of emotion.

This rustic Yorkshire barn setting, tucked into the hills above the Wharfe Valley, created a calm and natural space for their vows and personal moments.

As a Leeds wedding photographer, I loved documenting these honest, unposed moments — the kind of storytelling that feels right at home in the Yorkshire countryside.

Portraits at The Chilli Barn, Otley — Wedding Photography in Yorkshire

After the ceremony, we moved upstairs into the barn where guests gathered for relaxed hellos and natural portraits, with the marquee set below ready for the wedding breakfast.

It naturally followed on from the ceremony and flowed into the next part of the day as Helen stepped into her wellies and, with umbrellas from the groomsmen, we headed outside into a warm summer shower for some planned portraits with their white horse, just beyond the fields near Chevin Forest.

As a Leeds wedding photographer, I love working in these kinds of moments — unposed, a little unpredictable, and shaped by whatever the Yorkshire weather decides to do.

The Wedding Breakfast & Speeches at The Chilli Barn, Otley — Wedding Photography in Yorkshire

With the marquee set below the barn, the wedding breakfast at The Chilli Barn felt warm, relaxed, and full of atmosphere. Glasses clinked, stories were shared, and the room filled with laughter as friends and family gathered together.

From emotional speeches to bursts of laughter across the tables, these are the kinds of moments that make Yorkshire barn weddings so special — honest, unposed, and full of personality.

As a Leeds wedding photographer, I love documenting these natural celebrations as they unfold — capturing the energy, connection, and quiet in-between moments that tell the real story of the day.

Evening Moments & Portraits in Chevin Forest — Wedding Photography in Yorkshire

As the day eased into the evening at The Chilli Barn, soft summer rain still lingered in the air. Rather than holding things back, it simply added to the atmosphere as the celebrations continued in a relaxed, natural way.

Earlier planned portraits in Chevin Forest Park became part of the flow of the day — with umbrellas in hand and a quiet sense of adventure, we stepped into the woodland near Otley for some final images with their white horse and the Yorkshire countryside as a backdrop.

As a Leeds wedding photographer, I love ending the day like this — unplanned in feeling, honest in emotion, and shaped by whatever the Yorkshire weather brings.

A Yorkshire Barn Wedding, Told Honestly

Every image from Helen & Adam’s day reflects how I approach wedding photography — personal, natural, and focused on real moments as they unfold. From the barn to the surrounding fields, the story was shaped by light, weather, and genuine connection.

Whether you’re planning a wedding near Otley, around Chevin Forest, or anywhere across West Yorkshire, I document celebrations as they happen — no awkward posing, no forced direction. Just space for the day to breathe and unfold naturally.
