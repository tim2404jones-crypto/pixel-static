Speeches, Laughter, and a Quiet Moment to Remember

As more friends and family gathered for the evening celebration, Gemma and Danny chose this special night to share their speeches. The rich, warm colours of The Lost & Found Leeds Club interior set the perfect backdrop — atmospheric and full of feeling.

Danny’s speech was heartfelt, especially his touching words for his son, who stood nearby alongside the bride and best man by the bar. Guests gathered close, listening with smiles and tears, soaking in the unmistakable joy of a Leeds wedding filled with love.

The best man’s speech brought more laughter and smiles, followed by a quiet, unforgettable moment — their young son stood up to speak, and the whole room hushed in support. The first dance came next, beautifully captured in the soft, moody evening light — a perfect close to a joyous Leeds wedding celebration.
