Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 Jan 2026

Welcome to Tim Christian Jones Photography. Your privacy is very important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information.

What Information I Collect

- Name, email address, phone number (when you contact me)

- Information about your wedding (venue, date, preferences)

- Website usage data (via cookies and analytics)

How I Use Your Information

- To respond to your enquiries

- To arrange and deliver your wedding photography services

- To improve my website and services

- To occasionally send updates (only if you have agreed)

Cookies

I use cookies to make the website work better and understand how visitors use it. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Sharing Your Information

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. I may share details with trusted partners only when needed to deliver services (for example, album suppliers).

How I Protect Your Information

I take reasonable steps to protect your information from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

Your Rights

You can request to see the personal data I hold about you, and ask for it to be updated or deleted at any time. Please contact me to make a request.
