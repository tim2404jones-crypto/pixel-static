Julia & Scott's Pennine Manor October Windy Wedding

Nestled in the rolling hills of West Yorkshire, Pennine Manor feels both secluded and perfectly accessible. Its charming façade and tranquil gardens provide a sense of calm, while being just a short drive from local towns.

The manor offers a variety of elegant spaces for ceremonies and celebrations, from light-filled suites to intimate lounges. This makes it ideal for couples seeking a venue that can comfortably host both small gatherings and larger receptions.

With its combination of character, flexibility, and countryside charm, Pennine Manor provides a beautiful canvas for a full wedding day.

Julia and Scott were absolute stars on their October wedding at Pennine Manor Hotel in Huddersfield. The wind was wild, yet the couple—and all their guests—bravely ventured onto the helipad for the planned group photo. Hats off to everyone for embracing the gusts!

We captured a few portraits and playful moments in the wind before heading back inside to enjoy the wedding breakfast celebrations.

Pennine Manor Hotel Weddings in Yorkshire

Pennine Manor weddings always have a wonderfully relaxed atmosphere, with wide Yorkshire views, changing light, and a real sense of space around the day.
