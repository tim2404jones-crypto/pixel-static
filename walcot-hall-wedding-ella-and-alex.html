Ella & Alex — Walcot Hall Wedding

Ella and Alex’s wedding at Walcot Hall unfolded with warmth, humour, and a real sense of ease. From the moment they arrived, the day felt unhurried — shaped by laughter, easy conversation, and the people closest to them.

Set within the grounds of this remarkable family-owned venue, the celebration moved naturally from one moment to the next, full of small exchanges and genuine connection that gave the day its character.
